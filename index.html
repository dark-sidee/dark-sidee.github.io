About Me & My Journey

Recommended Books for CMI Preparation

Since I went through the rigorous prep phase myself, here are some books I highly recommend for anyone aiming for CMI or ISI:

  • Test of Mathematics at the 10+2 Level (TOMATO) - The absolute gold standard for ISI and CMI prep.
  • Challenge and Thrill of Pre-College Mathematics - Excellent for building a strong foundation in geometry, number theory, and combinatorics.
  • Excursion in Mathematics - Highly recommended for Olympiad-level problem solving.
  • Problem-Solving Strategies by Arthur Engel - A brilliant resource for advanced mathematical techniques and brain-teasers.
  • Higher Algebra by Hall and Knight - Classic text for strengthening algebra concepts.
